Toucan

Toucan Learning, in partnership with Think Fund, is offering
a free 7-week online Competition Math Camp from June 19
to August 4. This program is designed for beginners and
aims to help students build a strong foundation in
competition math, covering topics relevant to AMC 8,
MATHCOUNTS, MOEMS, and essential concepts for
AMC 10—and even introducing material potentially useful for
USACO. With small class sizes for personalized attention,
the camp is completely free and recommended for students
in 4th to 6th grade, though all are welcome.
Details about the class will be shared after registration.
